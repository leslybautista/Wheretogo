About the project

WhereToGo · spatial travel intelligence

A radial recommendation engine for European travel. Pick where you're starting from, when you'd like to go, and what matters — time, cost, carbon, curiosity — and WhereToGo plots cities by composite match against a real fare-and-duration cache.

The map

Each ring is a band of match quality — the inner ring holds the best fits, the outer band the longest reaches. Every destination is plotted on its true bearing from your origin city, so direction has geographic meaning.

The scoring

Four normalized factors — travel time, cost, CO₂, and popularity — combine into a single score. Adjust the weights from 0–100% in Advanced personalization, or pick a preset like Sustainable, Cheapest, or Hidden Gems.

The data

Flight fares and durations come from a Travelpayouts/Amadeus cache rebuilt monthly. Train data is layered in from DB / ÖBB / SNCF Sparpreis. Each destination card is filled with Wikivoyage highlights and city enrichment metadata.

Smart Choice

When both a train and a flight are viable, WhereToGo picks the lower-carbon option if your CO₂ weight is meaningful — otherwise it picks the faster one. Open any city card to compare both modes side by side.
